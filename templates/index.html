What it does

Converts Columbia-related emails into a weekly roadmap: Top 5 events (no calendar conflicts, not in the past) plus course / professor / school notices in a clean, readable format.
  • Filters emails that mention Columbia, SIPA, or Canvas (subject/snippet/body/sender domain).
  • Uses an LLM to classify: campus events, lectures/talks, course requirements, and school notices.
  • Extracts time, location, RSVP link, and gives a short English summary.
  • Lets you add a selected event directly to Google Calendar.
