What you get

After you sign in with Google, the app scans Columbia-related emails (Columbia/SIPA/Canvas or columbia.edu senders), classifies them with an LLM, and produces a clean dashboard:
  • Events: all eligible upcoming events ranked by AI relevance (past events and excluded keywords removed).
  • Conflict handling: events that conflict with your calendar are labeled CONFLICT and placed last.
  • One-click actions: open registration links and add events to Google Calendar.
  • Notices: course/professor/school notices grouped separately (not mixed).
  • Deadlines: if a notice contains a deadline, you can add a non-blocking reminder to your calendar (defaults to 9:00 AM if only a date is given).
